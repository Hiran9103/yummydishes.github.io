Weli Thalapa

INGREDIENTS

two cups of rice flour
two cups of coconut grated
half a cup of grated jaggery
half a cup of water
Ground cardamom, 1/2 teaspoon
A small amount of salt
Banana leaves to wrap
HOW TO COOK

To prepare Welithalapa, mix two cups of rice flour, grated coconut, and grated jaggery, each dissolved in half a cup of water. Additionally, add a pinch of salt and half a tsp of ground cardamom. Combine into a dense dough, form into tiny cylinders or ovals, and then cover with parchment paper or banana leaves. Cook by steaming for 20 to 25 minutes. Before serving, let cool, then remove the paper or leaves to reveal the mouthwatering, classic Sri Lankan dessert. Savor the delicious fusion of jaggery, coconut, and rice flavors at Welithalapa!
