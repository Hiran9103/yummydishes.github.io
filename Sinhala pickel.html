Sinhala Pickel/Sinhala Achcharu

INGREDIENTS(for 4 persons)

Two cups of finely chopped mixed vegetables (carrots)
About 15 red onions
10 green chilies(without cutting)
1/4 cup finely chopped ginger
1/4 cup finely chopped garlic
One tablespoon of seeds from black mustard
One-third cup of fenugreek seeds
One tablespoon of seeds for fennel
One teaspoon of seeds from cumin
One teaspoon of powdered turmeric
One teaspoon of chili powder (adjust to your desired level of     spice)
Half a cup of vinegar
HOW TO COOK

chop vegetables and combine them with red onions, green chilies, ginger, and garlic. Roast and grind mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, fennel seeds, and cumin seeds to make a spice blend. Combine the vegetables with the spice blend, turmeric powder, and chili powder. To make a pickling liquid, heat mustard oil and add vinegar, sugar, and salt. Pour the liquid over the vegetable mixture, making sure it is completely covered. Allow it to marinate for a few hours or overnight to bring out the flavors. Season with salt and sugar to taste. Store the pickle in sterilized jars and serve as a tasty condiment with meals.
