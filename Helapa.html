Helapa

INGREDIENTS

one cup of rice flour
one cup of coconut grated
Half a cup of grated or finely chopped jaggery
1/4 tsp powdered cardamom
A small amount of salt
Kenda tree papers to wrap
Contact us on hirankavindra@gmail.com
HOW TO COOK

Add 1/4 tsp powdered cardamom and a small pinch of salt to 1 cup each of rice flour, grated coconut, and finely chopped jaggery to make Helapa. Combine with water to form a thick dough. Form portions into flat discs and use toothpicks or twine to secure them while encasing them in banana leaves or Kenda tree papers. To cook, steam for 15 to 20 minutes. Let cool completely before serving. Savor this classic Sri Lankan dessert with its distinctive flavor combination!
